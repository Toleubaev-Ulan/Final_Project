Delivery

Delivery across Almaty

Our own staff of couriers and the constant availability of a full range of goods allow us to deliver orders without long expectations and delays!

Delivery cost

Delivery cost - 1200 tenge! With an order amount over 10,000 tenge - delivery is Free!

Delivery methods

Self-pickup - receipt of goods at the office of the company, within 5 days after the order status is changed to "Available".

Standard courier delivery (the cost of delivery within the city will cost - 1000tg), - to the address specified when placing an order. Delivery is carried out at a time and day convenient for the client, if there are free couriers and goods in the warehouse of the city, within which the delivery is carried out.

Express courier delivery - delivery of goods on the day of ordering, within 4 hours, when placing an order before 16:00 and the availability of free couriers and goods in the warehouse of the online store.

Delivery through Kazpost JSC (across Kazakhstan) - delivery of items to the post office of the settlement specified in the order (Regular mail - from 2 to 10 days).

Delivery by cash on delivery through Kazpost JSC (across Kazakhstan) - delivery of items to the post office of the settlement specified in the order with payment upon receipt (from 2 to 10 days).

Free delivery through Kazpost JSC (across Kazakhstan) - free delivery of items to the post office of the specified in the settlement (from 2 to 10 days) weighing less than 10 kg containing goods worth over 50,000 tenge.

Courier delivery DPD (across Kazakhstan) - delivery of items to the address specified in the order, to the entrance door in regional centers and some cities (from 2 to 10 days).

The standard period for the reserve of unpaid goods is 5 days, for paid goods - 30 days, longer periods are negotiated individually;

With our Love

We will deliver your order on any day convenient for you, including weekends and holidays! When ordering in the morning, delivery on the day of order is possible (subject to availability of couriers)!
